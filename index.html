About Tathva

From a technical symposium in 2000, to a technical fest in 2002 and a techno-managerial fest since 2007, Tathva has come a long way. This October, it is back again with 18 years of rich experience. What started out as the brainchild of a few curious minds, is now an emotion carried by those in and out of the college. It is also hailed as one of the biggest techno-managerial fests in South India. With bigger and better lectures, workshops, competitions and pronites, it attracts the best talent and intellect from all over the country, to those who wish to enhance and exhibit their knowledge.

Not limiting itself to just that, Tathva is also an open platform to anyone with technical and managerial aptitude, thereby expanding its horizons and exploring new opportunities. Brace yourselves for yet another amazing season of Tathva, this time bustling with energy and filled with an incredible amount of talent, seeking to find the best of the best, amongst us.
