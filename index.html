ABOUT

From a technical symposium in 2000, to a technical fest in 2002 and a techno-managerial fest since 2007, Tathva has come a long way. This October, it is back again with 18 years of rich experience. What started out as the brainchild of a few curious minds, is now an emotion carried by those in and out of the college. It is also hailed as one of the biggest techno-managerial fests in South India. With bigger and better lectures, workshops, competitions and pronites, it attracts the best talent and intellect from all over the country, to those who wish to enhance and exhibit their knowledge.
Not limiting itself to just that, Tathva is also an open platform to anyone with technical and managerial aptitude, thereby expanding its horizons and exploring new opportunities. Brace yourselves for yet another amazing season of Tathva, this time bustling with energy and filled with an incredible amount of talent, seeking to find the best of the best, amongst us.

WHY SPONSOR US?

Hailed as one of the biggest technical fests in South India, Tathva is an annual techno-management festival, organized by the National Institute of Technology Calicut. What started out as a small-scale technical festival during the REC era, has now grown, attracting about 10,000 students in over 60 enthralling events. It is a four day event that has grabbed the attention of techwizards and enthusiasts from over 900 colleges in the 16 years since its inception.

Students from various schools and colleges eagerly await every year for the month of October, when this prestigious festival is conducted. Aimed at inspiring innovation, technical interest among students, and awareness among the public, the event hosts lectures, seminars, workshops, competitions, paper presentations, exhibitions, quizzes, model displays and robotics events - all of which have had magnificent participation over the years. Events like Aavishkar, Wheels, Samasya, Blitzkrieg, and the resplendent Proshows have ensured that Tathva holds its audience spellbound every year. Not only does it provide the technologically savvy students a platform to showcase their skills, it also feeds the hungry minds of eager learners with information and knowledge.
